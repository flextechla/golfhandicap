Help & Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Golf Handicap?

A handicap is a numerical measure of a golfer's potential ability. It allows players of different skill levels to compete fairly. A lower handicap indicates a more skilled player.

Where do I find Rating and Slope?

These are located on your scorecard, usually next to the Tee Box names (Blue, White, Red, etc.).

How is my Index calculated?

This app uses the World Handicap System (WHS) logic. We take your "Score Differentials" from your most recent rounds. Specifically:

  • If you have 20+ rounds, we average the best 8 of your last 20.
  • For fewer rounds, we use a sliding scale (e.g., best 3 of last 10).
  • We then multiply that average by 0.96 to get your Index.
