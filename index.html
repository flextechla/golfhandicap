❓ Help & Support

How is my handicap calculated?

Your Handicap Index is calculated using the World Handicap System (WHS) formula. It takes the best 8 of your last 20 score differentials, averages them, and multiplies by 0.96. Score differential = (Score - Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating.

How do groups work?

Groups let you compete with friends on a private leaderboard. Create a group to get an invite code, then share that code with friends. You can be in multiple groups and switch between them to see different leaderboards.

How do I add a course?

Go to Settings → Manage Courses. You can add any course with its rating and slope. These values are typically found on the course's scorecard or website. Once added, all users can select the course when posting scores.

Why isn't my handicap showing?

You need at least 3 rounds with valid course rating and slope to calculate a handicap index. Make sure all your rounds have these values filled in.
