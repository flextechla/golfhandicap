Frequently Asked Questions

How is my handicap calculated?
We use the World Handicap System (WHS). Your handicap index is calculated by averaging the best differentials from your recent rounds (best 8 of your last 20 rounds, or best 50% if you have fewer rounds), then multiplying by 0.96.

What is a Score Differential?
Score Differential = (Gross Score - Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating

How many rounds do I need?
You can establish a handicap with as few as 3 rounds, but 20 rounds gives the most accurate index.

What if the course I play isn't listed?
You can add any course in Settings → Manage Courses. You'll need the course rating and slope rating, which are typically found on the scorecard or course website.

Can I play solo or do I need a group?
You can track your handicap solo! Groups are optional and let you compare scores with friends.

How do I invite friends to my group?
Share your group code found in the Groups tab. Friends enter this code to join your leaderboard.
