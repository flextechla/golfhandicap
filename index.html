❓ Help & Support

How is my handicap calculated?

Your Handicap Index is calculated using the World Handicap System (WHS) formula. It takes the best 8 of your last 20 score differentials, averages them, and multiplies by 0.96. Score differential = (Score - Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating.

How do groups work?

Groups let you compete with friends on a private leaderboard. Create a group to get an invite code, then share that code with friends.

How do I add a course?

Go to Settings → Manage Courses. Add any course with its rating and slope from the scorecard.

Why isn't my handicap showing?

You need at least 3 rounds with valid course rating and slope to calculate a handicap index.
